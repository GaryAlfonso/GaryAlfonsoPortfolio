About Gary The Bard

Gary The Bard, better known as Gary Alfonso is an artist.
His main medium of art is music composition, but he is
quite fond of creation and dabbles in many different
art forms. He aspires to become a game designer, or to
work as a creative director for a media company, such as
Disney.
