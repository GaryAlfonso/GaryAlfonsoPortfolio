Here is where you can find some of my game designs!!

Half Empty

Half Empty is a minimalist 2D platformer that
explores how feelings of self doubt affect the
creative process. The game is releasing on PC
in April 2017.

The Binding

The Binding is a Virtual Reality hack and slash experience.
The game has challenging combat in the vein of Dark Souls.
The purpose of the game is for the player to collect a
series of diary entries so that they may piece together
the storyline. The game is releasing on PC in April 2017

Sincerely, Him

Sincerely, Him is a a minamalist 2D platformer that .
tells the story of an ink blot that is attempting to
escape an apology note. The game was released on PC
in 2016. An updated repolished edition is due for
release in 2017.
